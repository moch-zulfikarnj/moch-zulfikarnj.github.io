Projects

  • Quadratic Number Field

    July 2023 – December 2023

    • Reading Project led by Gaurish Korpal (Ph.D. Candidate at the University of Arizona) focusing on understanding the Quadratic Number Field as an approach to learn Diophantine Equation
    • Keywords: Quadratic Number Field, Diophantine Equation, Number Theory
    • Related Link: DRP-NT Report

  • On the Topological Indices of Zero Divisor Graphs of Some Commutative Rings

    March 2021 – July 2022

    • Research Project led by Mrs. Intan Muchtadi-Alamsyah and continued by Fariz Maulana as his master's thesis to find topological indices of the zero divisor graph of some commutative rings
    • Keywords: Zero Divisor Graph, Commutative Ring, Topological Indices
    • Related Link: Abstract

  • Exploration of Mutually Unbiased Biangular Regular Set over Butson-Type BH(n, 3) on Constructing Association Scheme

    Aug 2019 – Aug 2020

    • Master Thesis supervised by Mr. Djoko Suprijanto, Ms. Pritta Etriana Putri, and Yusuf Hafidh focused on exploration of the possibility to construct an association scheme based on the mutually biangular regular set using Hadamard matrix of type BH(n,3)
    • Keywords: Biangular Regular Set, Association Scheme, Hadamard Matrix
    • Related Link:Abstract

  • Jacobson Graph over Zn

    Aug 2018 – May 2019

    • Undergraduate Thesis led by Ms. Intan Muchtadi-Alamsyah focused on exposition of Jacobson graph over some commutative rings such as modulo ring and find all characteristics
    • Keywords: Ring Theory, Graph Theory, Elementary Number Theory
    • Related Link: Paper
